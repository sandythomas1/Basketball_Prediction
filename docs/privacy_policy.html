Signal Sports Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Signal Sports collects only the data required to operate app features like authentication, profile setup, predictions, and AI chat usage limits.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email, display name, profile photo (if provided).
  • Usage data: feature usage events and AI chat usage counters.
  • Subscription status: free/pro tier and entitlement state.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate users and secure access to app features.
  • Enforce daily free limits and unlock Pro entitlements.
  • Improve reliability and monitor service performance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We use service providers required to run the app (Firebase, Google Cloud, RevenueCat, and Google Play Billing).

Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active or as needed to provide features and comply with legal obligations.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@signalsports.app
